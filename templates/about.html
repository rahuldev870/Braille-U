About Braille World

Empowering Accessibility Through Technology

Our Mission

Braille World is dedicated to making digital content accessible to everyone, regardless of their visual abilities. We believe that technology should break down barriers, not create them.

What We Do

Our comprehensive web application provides three essential tools for Braille conversion:

Text to Braille

Convert typed or spoken text to Braille with voice recognition

Image to Braille

Extract text from images using OCR and convert to Braille

Braille to Text

Convert Braille images back to readable text

Key Features

  • Multi-language Support: Hindi and English text processing
  • Voice Recognition: Advanced speech-to-text conversion
  • Text-to-Speech: Read aloud functionality for accessibility
  • OCR Technology: Extract text from images with high accuracy
  • Responsive Design: Works seamlessly on all devices
  • Real-time Conversion: Live Braille conversion as you type

Our Commitment

We are committed to continuous improvement and innovation in accessibility technology. Our platform is designed to be user-friendly, reliable, and accessible to users of all technical skill levels.

Technology Stack

Built with modern web technologies including Flask, Bootstrap, advanced OCR capabilities, and Web Speech API integration to ensure a smooth and reliable user experience.

"Technology should be a bridge to opportunity, not a barrier to access."
